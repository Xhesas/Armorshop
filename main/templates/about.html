About the Atlantic Armor Shop

The Atlantic Armor Shop (originally , lit. the store of armor and swords) is an atlantic company registered in Alnaŋaemak. It is lead by Namnak Xhesas and funded by the government of Enkavak while all of the companies profit is going to the government. The shop emerged from the trading culture in Alnaŋaemak and became more established overtime selling goods to far foreign countries, high officials and opening multiple other locations.
